My Story

Where it all began…

For years, my hands have been kept busy making cozy pieces exclusively for my family and friends. Seeing the joy, comfort, and warmth those handmade items brought to the people I love most is the greatest feeling in the world. Eventually, surrounded by support (and quite a bit of yarn!), I realized I wanted to share that same feeling with a wider community. That is how Cozy Crafts by Nina was born. Every single item I make is infused with that same childhood wonder, a modern dedication to intricate detail, and a whole lot of love. I am so incredibly excited to pass a piece of that coziness on to you! Every item is made to order and custom made by me, I will chat with you about the color, sizing and other details needed to give you 100% satisfaction with the product.

Behind the Scenes

How a Piece is Made

1

Inspiration & Design

Every piece starts with an idea — a color palette, a shape, a feeling. I sketch it out and select the perfect yarn from my collection.

2

Crafting by Hand

Hook in hand, I work stitch by stitch, row by row. Some pieces take an afternoon; others take days. Every loop is placed with purpose.

3

Finishing & Quality Check

I block, weave in ends, and inspect each piece carefully. If it doesn't meet my standards, it's back to the hook — no exceptions.

4

Wrapped with Love

Your order is packed in tissue paper with a handwritten note. Because every package should feel like a little gift, no matter what's inside.
